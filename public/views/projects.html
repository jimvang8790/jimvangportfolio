STEP-UP Check-In

STEP-UP train and place hundreds of Minneapolis youth in paid internships with top Twin Cities employers. Part of the City of Minneapolis STEP-UP program.

Step-Up Check-In, will allow students to give weekly feedback on their work experiences via their mobile devices. Application administrators will be able to access and analyze this data through the application dashboard and provide additional support to students as needed based on their responses.

Feel free to visit achievempls.org to learn more about this awesome program.

Angler Wrangler

Angler Wrangler is a MEAN stack web application allowing users to track what type of fish they caught and where they caught it.

Anglers can now keep track of their catches by utilizing Google Map's API allowing them to mark their exact location where they caught that prize fish. Users can also upload a photo of their catch along with some detailed information about that catch.

Start wrangling today at anglerwrangler.com to set up your free account today.
